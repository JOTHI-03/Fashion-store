About Glam Me Up


We keep the reader at the heart of everything we do,
and we like to think we know her well.
She is hard-working within her respective industry,
she enjoys the finer things in life like a fancy hotel or a good restaurant,
but is too busy to research which ones are right for her.


She has an international mindset and wants to see the world and
all it has to offer, whilst in a pretty pair of heels and
the perfect red manicure. We write for her,
with a goal of inspiring luxury living in every way possible
